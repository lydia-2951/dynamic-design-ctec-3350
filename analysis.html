Dynamic Design

SWOT Analysis

Internal Strengths Weaknesses
  • Mansfield Gardens known for educating customers on plant care and valuing customer needs
    • Emphasize plant care education on the website.
    • Use the About page to showcase the friendliness and helpfulness of the staff.
  • A lot of quality images already present on the website
    • Organize to increase the website's visual appeal.
  • Website characterizes Mansfield Gardens well
    • Keep phrases like "One-stop shop..." and use similar verbiage to make the positive characterization even more prominent.
  • Website features a newsletter with exclusive coupons
    • Revise the newsletter to serve as one of the benefits of joining the new loyalty program.
  • Web visibility of the website is low
    • Update keywords to optimize presence in major search engines.
  • Website design is outdated
    • Repair or remove the broken link on the Home page.
    • Update design and branding to better appeal to millennials.
    • Add a Google Maps link that accompanies the store address.
  • Website's message is unclear
    • Tie the text of the website together with a clear message inviting users to visit Mansfield Gardens.
    • Increase text size and contrast for better readability.
    • Add a two- or three-sentence description to products that do not already have one.
    • Increase the prominence of the company logo on the website.
  • Long Home page and long Plants and Products page make it easy to miss some of the content on the site
    • "Chunk" content into more distinct categories on more pages.
    • Reduce size of excessively large images.
External Opportunities Threats
  • "Plant culture" has become trendy and has had a resurgence in popularity because of social media
    • Capitalize on this trend by using related keywords to optimize presence in major search engines.
  • Millennials (the target audience) enjoy caring for indoor plants
    • Promote easy-care indoor and low-light plants.
    • Integrate mobile-first design to better appeal to millennials.
    • Add stock images to the website depicting people in the millennial age range caring for plants.
  • Workers are returning to offices after working from home
    • Promote indoor plants and new office plant care service on the website.
  • Larger competitors, currently with greater presence (Calloway's, Home Depot, Lowe's, etc.)
    • Use the messaging and style of the website to help Mansfield Gardens settle into a unique niche as a local, smaller, more trustworthy, and more ecologically friendly alternative.
  • Current supply chain issues due to COVID-19
    • Promote locally-grown plants and locally-sourced products.
  • COVID-19 might make customers hesitate to visit Mansfield Gardens in person
    • Promote the curbside pickup option more prominently on the Home page.
    • Give each product featured on the website a label indicating whether or not it is currently in stock, so users interested in curbside pickup will know what is available.
